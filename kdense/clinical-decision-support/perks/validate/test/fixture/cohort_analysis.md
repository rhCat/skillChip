# Patient Cohort Analysis: Biomarker-Stratified NSCLC

## Cohort Characteristics
We analyzed 45 patients (n = 45) stratified by biomarker expression level.

## Biomarker Stratification
Patients were grouped by tumor proportion score into high and low strata.

## Outcomes
Median PFS differed between strata (HR = 0.45, 95% CI 0.30-0.70, p < 0.001).

## Statistical Analysis
Kaplan-Meier estimates with log-rank test and Cox proportional-hazards regression.

## Clinical Implications
We strongly recommend continued therapy in the high-expression stratum [1] (GRADE 1A) per ASCO guidance.
